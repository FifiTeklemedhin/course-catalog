Theoretical and Empirical Perspective on Entrepreneurship: Economics and Finance (124076)
Josh Lerner
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: W 0529 PM - 0829 PM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
Entrepreneurship is a complex phenomenon that has attracted little academic attention. This course explores the emerging work in this area. Students taking the course for credit will be expected to complete two referee reports and a paper.
Course Notes: Offered jointly with the Business School as 4350.
