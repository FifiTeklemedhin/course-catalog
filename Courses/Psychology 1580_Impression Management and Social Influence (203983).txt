         This course will focus on the private and public parts of the self –what people keep to themselves versus show others. Specifically, we will focus on the role of impression management in determining behavior—how does the presence of other people change one's behavior? What are the ways in which people manage the impressions others form of them? What is at stake when people try to make certain impressions on others? We will examine cognitive, emotional, and relational precursors in professional and social contexts. We will cover issues pertaining to professional success, forming and maintaining relationships, and engaging risky behavior, as well as what happens
