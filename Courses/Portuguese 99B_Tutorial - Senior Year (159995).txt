Portuguese 99B
Tutorial - Senior Year (159995) HARVARD UNIVERSITY
For honors seniors writing a thesis. Successful completion of two terms of Portuguese 99 is required of all thesis-track honors concentrators. Students who do not complete a thesis are required to submit a substantial paper in order to receive either half course or full course credit.
This course is taught by members of the Department.
Satisfactory completion of a junior tutorial and prior faculty approval of proposed thesis topic. Successful completion of one term of Portuguese 99 is required of all honors concentrators. To enroll, see course head.
Prerequisite: French 98 OR Italian 98 OR Portuguese 98 OR Spanish 98 OR Romance Studies 98 OR Latin American Studies 98
