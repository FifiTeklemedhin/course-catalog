           This course will examine psychological and physical health from the perspective of Positive Psychology. A major focus will be on mindfulness theory and its relationship to stress/coping; illness/wellness; decision-making; placebos. The medical model, the bio social model, and a unified mind/body model will be compared to examine their relationship to achieving resilience.
Recommended Prep: The Psychology Department requires completion of Science of Living Systems 20 or the equivalent of introductory psychology (e.g. Psych AP=5
