2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: T 0100 PM - 0259 PM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: 18
Examines human creativity from three perspectives: a) empirical research sources, b) case studies of eminent creative achievers, and c) ourselves as creative subjects. Topics include the definition and measurement of creativity, the creative process, the neuroscience of creativity, the creative personality, the role of family life and culture in creativity, the relationship of creativity to IQ, gender differences, and the relationship of creativity to psychopathology. This is a junior tutorial.
Class Notes: Preference to juniors in MBB tracks or MBB secondary field. Additional Course Attributes:
