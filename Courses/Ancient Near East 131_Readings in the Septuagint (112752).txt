         Aims to increase facility with Septuagint Greek by reading representative prose portions of the Septuagint and studying the peculiarities of the grammar inductively. The fundamentals of Hellenistic Greek will be reviewed as necessary.
Course Notes: Offered jointly with the Divinity School as 4215. Recommended Prep: One year of Greek.
