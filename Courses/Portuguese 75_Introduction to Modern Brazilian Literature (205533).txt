This course is an introductory survey of Brazilian literature from the proclamation of the Republic (1889) to the present. It questions how some of the most salient historical, political and cultural events in Brazil have been represented through diverse literary forms including novels, short stories and poetry, in addition to visual media, like photography and film. Readings by Machado de Assis, Mario de Andrade, Cecília Meireles, João Guimarães Rosa, Nelson Rodrigues, Clarice Lispector, among others.
