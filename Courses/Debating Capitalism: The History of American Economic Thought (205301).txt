         How does capitalism work? Does it work? Could it work better? Ultimately, what sort of economy should the United States be pursuing? This course wrestles with how American thinkers—both popular and academic—have
HARVARD UNIVERSITY 9/4/2017 0:39 AM
answered these questions since the founding of the nation. From the (neo) classical conception of political economy, progressive reform, and feminist economics to Keynesianism, African American economic thought, and the rise of laissez-faire, "Debating Capitalism" gives students the opportunity to engage in the swirling varieties of economic ideologies and political programs that have taken root in America over the past two centuries.
