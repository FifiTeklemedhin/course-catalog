MCB 351 is a refresher course in the Responsible Conduct of Research which must be completed by graduate students in the MCO PhD program every 4 years, and fulfills the National Institutes of Health (NIH) and National Science Foundation (NSF) requirements for formal Responsible Conduct of Research (RCR) instruction.
