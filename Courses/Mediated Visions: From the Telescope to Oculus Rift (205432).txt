         This course examines the use of imagining techniques from the Scientific Revolution to the present day. How has visual imagery affected the construction of scientific knowledge across varying fields? How did new technologies shape the types of images scientists produce? What is the relation between image-making, scientific research, and formal scientific theories? And how have new technologies impacted theories of physiology, astronomy, and mathematics? By following a different technology each week and drawing upon a rich array of cases from the
