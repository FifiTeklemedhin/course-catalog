Economics 10B
Principles of Economics (109894)
N. Mankiw
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: MWF 1200 PM - 1259 PM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
Economic growth, inflation, unemployment, the business cycle, the financial system, international capital flows and trade imbalances, and the impact of monetary and fiscal policy. The Department of Economics strongly encourages students considering a concentration in economics to take both Ec10a and Ec10b in their freshman year. These courses are required for all economics concentrators and are prerequisites for higher level courses in economics.
Course Notes:
Recommended Prep: Additional Course Attributes:

Ec 10a is recommended but not required.
