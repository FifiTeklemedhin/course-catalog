Knowing Animals: Buddhist Resources For a New Interspecies Ethics (205390)
Janet Gyatso
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: TR 1130 AM - 1259 PM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: 20
This course will study exemplary new writing in the "post-human" turn, works on animal ethics, animal anthropology, and stories and videos of experiences with animals. Buddhist resources will include cosmology, the epistemology of seeing, and the epistemology of compassion.
