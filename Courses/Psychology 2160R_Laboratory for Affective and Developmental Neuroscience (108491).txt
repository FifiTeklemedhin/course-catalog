Conduct research on emotion processing and/or adolescent socioemotional development, incorporating methods of cognitive neuroscience including functional brain imaging (fMRI). Read and discuss current issues in the fields of affective, cognitive, and developmental neuroscience.
For information on this lab, contact Megan Garrad at andl@g.harvard.edu.
For undergraduates, Science of Living Systems 20 or the equivalent of introductory psychology (e.g. Psych AP=5 or IB =7 or Psyc S-1) and at least one foundational course from MCB 80, MCB 81, PSY 14, PSY 15, PSY 16, PSY 18 or Science of Living Systems 15.
