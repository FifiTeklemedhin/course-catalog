 Introduces students to the classical orthography and basics of morphology and syntax of [western] Armenian. Emphasis on the development of the four skills (reading, speaking, listening, and writing). Samples of modern (contemporary) and classical styles of writing introduced into basic syllabus, and audio-visual material from the contemporary Armenian media. Required textbook: tba. Students must complete both terms of this course (Armenian AA and AB) within the same academic year in order to receive credit.
