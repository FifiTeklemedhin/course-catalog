         After an introduction to the basic techniques of dynamics and ergodic theory, the course will develop applications, ranging from classical to more recent developments. Possible topics: Margulis super-rigidity and arithmeticity of lattices in Lie groups, measure rigidity theorems (Ratner, Benoist-Quint), dynamical systems in algebraic geometry.
