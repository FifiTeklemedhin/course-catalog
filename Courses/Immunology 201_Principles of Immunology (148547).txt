Comprehensive core course in basic immunology, providing an intensive and in-depth examination of the cells and molecules of the immune system. Special attention is given to the experimental approaches that led to the discovery of the general principles of immunology.

Class Notes:
Recommended Prep:
Intended for students who have had prior exposure to immunology on the undergraduate level. In the absence of such exposure, students must obtain the permission of the Course Director. Offered jointly with the Medical School as IM 702.0.
Classes begin on August 30, 2017 through November 30, 2017. First Class on August 30 will be held at 1:30pm in 100A Modell.
A background in genetics and biochemistry strongly recommended.
