Italian 99B
Tutorial - Senior Year (159978) HARVARD UNIVERSITY
For honors seniors writing a thesis. Successful completion of two terms of Italian 99 is required of all thesis-track honors concentrators. Students who do not complete a thesis are required to submit a substantial paper in order to receive either half course or full course credit.
This course is taught by members of the Department.
Satisfactory completion of a junior tutorial and prior faculty approval of proposed thesis topic.
Prerequisite: Italian 98 OR equivalent.
