          Early-Stage Research and Discussions on Economic Development (110160)
Nathan Nunn Melissa Dell Emily Breza
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: M 0300 PM - 0429 PM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: n/a
Participants discuss recent research in economic development and present their own work in progress. Popularly known as the Development Tea.
Course Notes: Primarily, but not exclusively, for doctoral students in economics who have passed their oral examinations.
