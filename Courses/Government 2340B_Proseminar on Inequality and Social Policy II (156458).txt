         The second doctoral seminar in the Inequality and Social Policy three-course sequence, this course considers the effects of policies and institutions in creating or reducing inequality in the US and other advanced democracies, as well as the reciprocal effects of inequality on political activity and policy choices.
Course Notes: Offered jointly with the Kennedy School as SUP-922. Co-taught with Alexander Keyssar.
