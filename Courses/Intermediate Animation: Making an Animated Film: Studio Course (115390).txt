Intermediate Animation: Making an Animated Film: Studio Course (115390)
Ruth S. Lingford
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: R 0100 PM - 0459 PM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: 12
This course offers returning animators a chance to extend and deepen skills and understanding of animation and to make a more substantial piece of work. Additional exercises encourage students to challenge themselves and explore a range of creative possibilities.
Interested students must attend first meeting of class to speak with teaching staff about enrollment procedures. This course has an additional weekly film screening, Fridays, 1-3 pm.
This class welcomes students with any previous animation/video/or filmmaking experience.
