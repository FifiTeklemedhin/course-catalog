Music 221R Section: 01
Current Issues in Music Theory (124044)
Christopher Hasty
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: TBD Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
What does Music Theory mean in the 21st century? What are the current debates? We examine recent and contemporary discussions in (and about) the field. We focus our attention on a single central work, Beethoven's Symphony No. 9, which has generated more discussion than any other.
