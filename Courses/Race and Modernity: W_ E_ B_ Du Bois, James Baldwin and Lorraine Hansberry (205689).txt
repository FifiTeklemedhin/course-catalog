Race and Modernity: W. E. B. Du Bois, James Baldwin and Lorraine Hansberry (205689)
Cornel West
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: TR 1100 AM - 1159 AM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
This course will examine the classic texts of the towering intellectual figures who exemplify profound interpretations of race and modernity. Works include Du Bois's The Souls of Black Folk, Dusk of Dawn, Baldwin's Notes of a Native Son, No Name in the Street and Hansberry's Raisin in the Sun and To Be Young, Gifted and Black.
