 Greek Ax is designed for students who have studied some Greek prior to enrollment at Harvard. Participants will review and consolidate their knowledge of Greek grammar and apply that knowledge to the reading of short texts. Students should leave the course able to read with the precision that is necessary to use primary sources in research.
