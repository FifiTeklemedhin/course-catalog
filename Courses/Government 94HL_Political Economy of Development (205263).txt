         Observes core topics of political economy in developing democracies, while also covering empirical methods for causal identification (e.g., difference-in-differences, regression discontinuity, instrumental variables). What is the role of institutions in explaining economic development? Why do communities fail at providing public goods? Why do voters often fail to hold their elected officials accountable? How are candidates able to circumvent electoral accountability though clientelism and vote buying? What is the role of media in promoting electoral accountability?
