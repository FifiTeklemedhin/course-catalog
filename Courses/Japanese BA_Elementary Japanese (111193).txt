This course aims to develop a basic foundation in modern Japanese leading to proficiency in the four language skills of speaking, listening, reading and writing. Emphasis is placed on the use of these skills to communicate effectively in authentic contexts of daily life. Mastery of hiragana, katakana, and approximately 86 Kanji (Chinese characters).
Class Notes: Class meets five times a week, additional discussion sections to be arranged.
