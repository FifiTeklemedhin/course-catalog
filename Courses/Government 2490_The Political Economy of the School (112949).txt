The Political Economy of the School (112949)
Paul Peterson
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: TR 0115 PM - 0229 PM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: 10
Identifies political and economic influences on education policy, expenditure levels, and governance. Examines classic and recent studies of selected school policies (class size, charters, vouchers, accountability, school organization, etc.), giving particular attention to research design and methodological issues as well as to theories and interpretations of institutions and processes.
