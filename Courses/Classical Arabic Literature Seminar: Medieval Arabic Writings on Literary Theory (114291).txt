Classical Arabic Literature Seminar: Medieval Arabic Writings on Literary Theory (114291)
Shady Nasser
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: R 0200 PM - 0459 PM HARVARD UNIVERSITY
9/4/2017 0:39 AM
Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
A survey of medieval Arabic writings on literary theory, from the early proponents of the Qaṣīda form and ʿamūd al-shiʿr to selected writings on muḥdath poetry. Works on both poetry and prose will be considered. Authors included: Ibn al-Muʿtazz, Ibn Qutayba, al-Marzūqī, al-Jurjānī, Ḥāzim al-Qarṭajannī, etc. Topics include: Qaṣīda form, Eloquence (faṣāḥa and balāghah), poetics (badīʿ, majāz, etc.) Qurʾān and inimitability.
Recommended Prep: Advanced knowledge of Classical Arabic. Additional Course Attributes:
Arabic 246
