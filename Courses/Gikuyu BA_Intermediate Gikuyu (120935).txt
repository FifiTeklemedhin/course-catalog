             Continuation of Gikuyu A. Gikuyu is a Bantu language spoken by Kenya's most populous ethnic group. The Gikuyu are among Africa's most recognized peoples because of the Mau Mau freedom fighters who were mainly Gikuyu. Students must complete the second term of this course (Gikuyu BB) within the same academic year in order to receive credit.
