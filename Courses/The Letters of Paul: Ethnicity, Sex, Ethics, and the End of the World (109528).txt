The Letters of Paul: Ethnicity, Sex, Ethics, and the End of the World (109528)
Laura Nasrallah
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: MW 1100 AM - 1159 AM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
This introductory course focuses on 1) the Pauline epistles in their first-century sociopolitical context, and their earliest interpretations; 2) recent trends in Pauline studies, including feminist and postcolonial interpretation, the New Perspective, and European philosophical treatments (Badiou, Zizek).
