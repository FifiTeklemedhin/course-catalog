Classic Texts and Current Trends in American Music. Explores the historiography of American music, with emphasis on recent developments. What is "American" music, and how does it fit into the world? The course cuts across time and deals with diverse genres, with explorations of race as a binding thread. Class projects will focus on local archives and resources, as well as the quickly growing realm of digitized sources.
