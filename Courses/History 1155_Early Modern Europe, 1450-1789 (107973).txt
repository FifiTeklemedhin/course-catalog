This survey course explores European history from the end of the Middle Ages to the eighteenth century. Topics include the Renaissance in Southern and Northern Europe, the Reformation and the wars of religion, the rise of absolutism, the Enlightenment, and the causes of the French Revolution. The course will introduce students to key figures (such as Machiavelli, Luther, Montaigne, Bacon, and Voltaire) as well as to topics in cultural and everyday history.
