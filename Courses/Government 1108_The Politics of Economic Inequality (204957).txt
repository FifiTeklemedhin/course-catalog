         This course will examine the causes and consequences of the rise in the inequality of the distribution of income and wealth in the United States, other industrialized democracies, developing and transition countries, and globally. We will review economic and political theories explaining increasing inequality, along with the implications of inequality for democracy, social and geographic mobility, and governance.
