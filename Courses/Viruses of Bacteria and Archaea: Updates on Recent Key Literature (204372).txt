Viruses of Bacteria and Archaea: Updates on Recent Key Literature (204372)
Max Nibert
2018 Spring (2 Credits) Schedule: TR 0130 PM - 0329 PM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: 8
In this course, we will expand our view, commonly focused on viruses of humans and sometimes other vertebrates, to viruses of bacteria and archaea. Emphasis will be placed on exciting new findings in the recent literature. Active student participation will be expected and essential for the success of this readings and discussions based course, which is intended for students with previous advanced background in virology
