Economics and Politics: The Foundations of Economics in Political Theory (118796)
Benjamin Friedman Richard Tuck
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: W 0200 PM - 0359 PM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
Offers graduate students in relevant disciplines the chance to study the historical origins of central ideas in modern economics and to discuss their philosophical character.
