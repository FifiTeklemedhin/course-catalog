State-Building (203483)
Daniel Ziblatt
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: M 0100 PM - 0259 PM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: 30
Investigates state-building in comparative perspective. With focus on developing and developed worlds, topics include: What is the state? What theoretical approaches best explain strong and weak states? What are consequences of these variations?
