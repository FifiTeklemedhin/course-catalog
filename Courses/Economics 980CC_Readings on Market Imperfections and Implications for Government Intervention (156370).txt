Economics 980CC
Readings on Market Imperfections and Implications for Government Intervention (156370)
Nathaniel Hendren
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: M 0200 PM - 0359 PM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: 18
This course focuses on rationales for and methods of government interventions in private markets. We cover various rationales, such as environmental externalities, fiscal externalities, and asymmetric information. And, we focus on various methods of intervention, such as taxes, regulation, direct government provision, mandates. We motivate our discussions using a combination of classic economics papers and more recent work focused on topical issues such as health insurance regulation and climate change. This s a junior tutorial.
