2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: TR 1000 AM - 1059 AM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
Continuation of Ba. Non-intensive introduction to modern Chinese pronunciation, grammar, conversation, reading, and writing for students with little or no background in the language. The course will provide students with a basic foundation in all four areas of Chinese language ability: listening, speaking, reading and writing.
