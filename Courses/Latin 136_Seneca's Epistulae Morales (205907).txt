The focus of this course will be in-depth reading of Seneca's Epistulae Morales in Latin, with emphasis on the intersection of philosophy and literature in the letters, as well as on their cultural and political context. Special attention will be given to Seneca's life and career, Roman Stoicism, and the unique style of these engaging epistles on how to live.
