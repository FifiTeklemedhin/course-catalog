Representation Theory, Geometry, and the Wonderful Compactification (204952)
Ana Balibanu
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: TR 0100 PM - 0229 PM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
An introduction to the construction and properties of the wonderful compactification, and to its geometry. Possible topics: the DeConcini-Procesi construction, Mumford's GIT, the Vinberg monoid and the total coordinate ring of a projective variety, toric varieties, spherical and wonderful varieties, Poisson geometry and the logarithmic cotangent bundle, Hamiltonian reduction.
