This course surveys spiritual practices and movements that have been labeled as "metaphysical," "esoteric," "occult," "harmonial," or "New Age." Beginning with colonial practices of astrology, alchemy, and spirit possession, we will consider Swedenborgianism, Spiritualism, Theosophy, New Thought, and their myriad descendants in the twentieth and twenty-first centuries.
Course Notes: The course will also feature field trips to two or three spiritual communities. Offered jointly with the Divinity School as 2360.
