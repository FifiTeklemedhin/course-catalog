         An introduction to Qur'anic exegesis and prophetic tradition (Hadith). A survey of different schools of Qur'anic interpretation within the Islamic tradition. The course introduces the discipline of Hadith and the role it plays in Qur'anic commentary. Topics include: Hadith, transmission, collections of sound Prophetic traditions, Sunni and Shii Hadith collections, Schools of Qur'anic Exegesis, Esoteric interpretation, Modern Interpretation, Qur'an, science and modernity.
