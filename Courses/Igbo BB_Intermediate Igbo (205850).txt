Igbo BB
Intermediate Igbo (205850)
John Mugane
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: TBD Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
A study of Igbo one of the three most widely spoken languages in Nigeria at the Intermediate level (Second year part 2). Contact hours supplemented by digital resources sessions. Emphasis on written expression, reading comprehension, and oral fluency. Students must complete both terms of this course (parts BA and BB) within the same academic year in order to receive full Intermediate Igbo course credit. Students taking Igbo BA in the Spring must note that Igbo BB is offered only in the Spring.
