Scientists don't just generate data; they also need to be able to present it during a lab meeting, talk, poster session, publication, or thesis.
Students will learn how to use the basic tools and features of Adobe Illustrator to tackle graphic needs and issues they may encounter. The course will cover topics such as: how to set up multipage documents; how to import and export data, drawings, and text; reading a journal's art directions; handling colors (color blindness, RGB vs CMYK), handling type; potential copyright issues; and stock art.
Class can be customized to students' needs and goals
