Joseph D. Harris
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: TR 0200 PM - 0329 PM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
An interactive introduction to problem solving with an emphasis on subjects with comprehensive applications. Each class will be focused around a group of questions with a common topic: logic, information, number theory, probability, and algorithms.
Mathematics 19b or 21b or 23a; or an equivalent background in mathematics. More importantly, students should have a broad mathematical curiosity and be eager to brainstorm during in-class problem solving sessions.
