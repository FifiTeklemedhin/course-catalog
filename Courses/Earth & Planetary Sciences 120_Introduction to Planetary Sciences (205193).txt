An overview of the key physical and chemical processes that occur on planetary bodies of the solar system and a survey of current topics of research.
Course Notes: This course fulfills the EPS sub-discipline requirement of Geology, Geophysics and Planetary Science. Given in alternate years.
Recommended Prep: Physics 15a or equivalent or permission of instructor. Introductory EPS class such as EPS 10 recommended.
