         A discussion of classical results and recent advances in the study of groups of diffeomorphisms of disks. Topics: the results of Smale and Hatcher, the relation to exotic spheres and smoothing theory, the relation to cobordism categories and the relation to algebraic K-theory. If time permits, a discussion of the relation to configuration space integrals.
