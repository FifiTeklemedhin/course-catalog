Provides an opportunity for students to engage in preparatory research and the writing of a senior thesis. Graded on a SAT/UNS basis as recommended by the thesis supervisor. The thesis is evaluated by the supervisor and by two additional readers.
Course Notes:
May be taken as a half course in either term; normally may not be taken for more than two terms. The Director of Undergraduate Studies will sign the student's study card once a faculty member has agreed in writing to supervise preparation of the thesis, and reaffirmed this agreement if the course is to be repeated. Applications may be obtained at Pierce Hall 110. Students should consult their advisers and concentration literature for further information and guidance.
