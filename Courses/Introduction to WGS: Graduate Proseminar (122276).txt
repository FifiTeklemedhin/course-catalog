         This course offers an introduction to theoretical themes in the study of women, gender, and sexuality. Special attention is given to recent critical debates that have (re)energized the field and that cross disciplinary boundaries. The course also addresses matters of professional development.
Course Notes: Will count as the Graduate Proseminar for the PhD secondary field requirement in WGS.
