         Psychology 2580r is a hands-on course in which students participate in all aspects of the research process, from the design and execution of empirical research studies to the analysis of data. Each student works closely with a graduate student or post-doctoral mentor who supervises the student's daily activities. Students are admitted to PSY 2580r only by permission.
