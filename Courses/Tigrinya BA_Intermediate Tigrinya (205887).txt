Schedule: TBD Enrollment Cap: n/a
A study of Tigrinya a major language spoken in Ethiopia at the Intermediate level (Second year part 1). Contact hours supplemented by language lab sessions. Emphasis on written expression, reading comprehension, and oral fluency. Students must complete both terms of this course (parts BA and BB) within the same academic year in order to receive full Intermediate Tigrinya course credit. Students taking Tigrinya BA in the Spring must note that Tigrinya BB is offered only in the Spring and must therefore wait for Spring to complete the course.
