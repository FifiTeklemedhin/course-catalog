A study of Sudanese Arabic the official and national working language in Sudan at the Intermediate level (Second year part 1). Contact hours supplemented by language lab sessions. Emphasis on written expression, reading comprehension, and oral fluency. Students must complete both terms of this course (parts BA and BB) within the same academic year in order to receive full Intermediate Sudanese Arabic course credit. Students taking Sudanese Arabic BA in the Spring must note that Sudanese Arabic BB is offered only in the Spring and must therefore wait for Spring to complete the course.
