Early Medieval History, Archaeology and Archaeoscience: Seminar (114862)
Michael McCormick
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: M 0300 PM - 0459 PM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
Joint philological analysis of Latin texts, archaeological and scientific evidence illuminating the fall of Rome and the origins of medieval Europe, culminating in a research paper.
Recommended Prep: Latin, with either German or French, is required. Normally History 2050 and or MS 101.
Additional Course Attributes:
History 205
