           A study of Haitian Creole the dominant official and native language of Haiti at the Intermediate level (Second year part 2). Contact hours supplemented by digital resources sessions. Emphasis on written expression, reading comprehension, and oral fluency. Students must complete both terms of this course (parts BA and BB) within the same academic year in order to receive full Intermediate Haitian Creole course credit. Students taking Haitian Creole BA in the Spring must note that Haitian Creole BB is offered only in the Spring.
