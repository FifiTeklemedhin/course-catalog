Incorporating both studio and seminar instruction, this intensive course will explore printmaking's history, trace its particular forms of intelligence, and test its future potential. The class will meet for three hours of studio and two hours of seminar/discussion per week. Assignments will include weekly readings, a short scholarly paper, and two
