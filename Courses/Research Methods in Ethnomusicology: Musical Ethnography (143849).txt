Research Methods in Ethnomusicology: Musical Ethnography (143849)
Kay Shelemay
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: TBD Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
An introduction to the theories and methods of ethnomusicological fieldwork, including changing conceptions of the research site, ethical concerns, interview techniques, the ethnography of musical performance, and data analysis and interpretation.
Course Notes: Individual research project required. Open to undergraduates with permission of instructor.
