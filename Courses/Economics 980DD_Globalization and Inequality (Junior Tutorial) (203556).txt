Economics 980DD
Globalization and Inequality (Junior Tutorial) (203556)
Pol Antras
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: W 0900 AM - 1059 AM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: 30
This course will overview recent trends in the distribution of income within and across countries and will discuss economic research aimed at understanding the role of globalization (trade integration, multinational activity, etc) in shaping these trends. The course will focus on analytical and statistical methods and therefore requires knowledge of calculus and econometrics.
