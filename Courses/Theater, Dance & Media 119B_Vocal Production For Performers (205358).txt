For students interested in working in theater, film and television, this course is a continued exploration of vocal production and speech skills. Building on concepts of breath, resonance, and articulation, this course will move on to explore support for vocal extremes, the International Phonetic Alphabet in preparation for dialect work, and the skills necessary for speaking Shakespeare with power and precision. TDM 119 is a suggested but not required prerequisite.
