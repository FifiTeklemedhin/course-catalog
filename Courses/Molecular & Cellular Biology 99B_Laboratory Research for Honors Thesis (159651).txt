For honors candidates writing a thesis in Molecular and Cellular Biology. Ordinarily may not be taken as a fifth course. Students must complete both terms of this course (A and B) in order to receive credit.
Ordinarily may not be taken as a fifth course. Indivisible for students enrolling in the fall term. Students intending to enroll in the fall are required to submit a written proposal to the instructor. Students may enter the course at midyear only with the permission of the instructor. The thesis proposal must be approved by the instructor and Head Tutor prior to enrolling in MCB 99.
