This course focuses on professional development and preparation for academic careers in literature and related fields as well as positions outside academe. It is open to all Harvard graduate students and is required of second- year Ph.D. students in Comparative Literature. Part one of a two-part series. Students must complete both terms of this course (parts A and B) within the same academic year in order to receive credit.
