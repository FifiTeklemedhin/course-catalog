Islander communities and their diaspora since the early twentieth century. This seminar aims to familiarize students with the historiography of the Asia/Pacific wars and encourages them to investigate the social, cultural, and political history of U.S. militarization, empire, and settler colonialism in Asia and the Pacific Islands via individual research projects. The course brings together primary source material and scholarly work from the fields of U.S. History, Asian American Studies, Pacific Islander American Studies, and East Asian Studies.
