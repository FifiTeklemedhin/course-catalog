         In this seminar, we will learn about current developments in vision and cognitive neuroscience research. Based on the students' research interests, we will select appropriate papers that will cover the background and ongoing research effort on particular research topics, including, but not limited to, attention, working memory, and object representation. We will discuss research papers using behavioral, human neuroimging and monkey neurophysiology methods. Students will also present their own ongoing work relevant to the research topics under discussion.
