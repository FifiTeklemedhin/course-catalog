Intergroup Relations: Graduate Research Seminar (205292)
James Sidanius
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap:
TBD 30
This seminar provides students with research experience concerning different forms of intergroup relations, including the social psychology of interracial and interethnic conflict and prejudice. Graduate students meet on a weekly basis to discuss ongoing research on psychological mechanisms involved in power, stereotyping, inequality, identity, and ideology.
