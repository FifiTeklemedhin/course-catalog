Pathologies of Description: Adalbert Stifter (205059)
John T. Hamilton
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: F 1000 AM - 1159 AM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: 30
The seminar engages in a close and critical reading of the major prose works of Adalbert Stifter within the broader context of Nineteenth-Century Literary Realism. Themes and tensions include: description and narration; verbal surface and depth; realism and allegory; convention and transgression; figuration and symptom.
