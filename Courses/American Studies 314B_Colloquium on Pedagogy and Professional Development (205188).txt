American Studies 314B
Colloquium on Pedagogy and Professional Development (205188)
Joyce Chaplin
2018 Spring (2 Credits) Schedule: M 0900 AM - 1029 AM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: n/a
Topics in pedagogy and professional development for third-year students in American Studies. Strongly recommended for American Studies G-3s, and open to others by permission of the instructor.
Students must complete both terms of this course (parts A and B) within the same academic year in order to receive credit.
