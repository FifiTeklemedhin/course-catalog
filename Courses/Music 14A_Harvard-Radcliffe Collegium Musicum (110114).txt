             Harvard-Radcliffe Collegium Musicum (SATB choir). Harvard's nationally acclaimed mixed choir, the Collegium performs a dynamic and innovative repertoire from classical masterpieces to new compositions by renowned composers as well as HRCM members. Through collaborative endeavors, tours, and community engagement, the ensemble fosters a passionate community of student musicians. The Collegium frequently combines with the Glee Club and Choral Society, as well as the Harvard-Radcliffe Orchestra, to perform large-scale works. Students must complete both parts of this course (parts A and B) within the same academic year in order to receive credit.
