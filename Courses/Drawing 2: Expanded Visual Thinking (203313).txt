         Drawing from life, image and imagination, this studio art course will focus on the medium of drawing and its many potentials. Drawing will be considered as installation, process and contemporary practice. Conceptual, technical and artistic visual skills will be explored and developed. Students will create a comprehensive body of work investigating personal interests based on ideas and concepts covered in class following each topic and assignment.
Course Notes: This course is open to anyone who has some experience with creative visual work.
Interested students must attend first meeting of class during shopping week to speak with teaching staff about course enrollment procedure.
