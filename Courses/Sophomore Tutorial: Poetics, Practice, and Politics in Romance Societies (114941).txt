This course introduces students to a range of analytical and theoretical approaches to "reading" fiction, poetry, film, and essays. We will pair critical writings and creative texts of importance to the Romance world to help students develop their own voice and analytical stance. Prepares students for advanced work in literary and cultural studies in Romance Languages and Literatures and related fields.
