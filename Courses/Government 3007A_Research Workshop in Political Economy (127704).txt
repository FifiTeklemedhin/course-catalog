             Intended for graduate students in the third year and above, this course welcomes scholarship of all types and on all aspects of political economy. Intended to provide a venue in which to develop and to debate work in progress. Part one of a two-part course; students must complete both terms of this course (parts A and B) within the same academic year in order to receive credit.
