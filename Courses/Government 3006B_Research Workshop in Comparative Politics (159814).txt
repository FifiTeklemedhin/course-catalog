Research Workshop in Comparative Politics (159814)
Timothy Colton
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: W 0400 PM - 0559 PM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: n/a
The workshop offers advanced graduate students an opportunity to present their work-in-progress, benefit from critiques of it, and discuss theoretical and methodological issues. Part two of a two part series. Students must complete both parts of this course (parts A and B) within the same academic year in order to receive credit.
Course Notes: Doctoral students from other departments and faculties admitted if space permits.
