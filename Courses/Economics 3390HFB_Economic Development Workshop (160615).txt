Fall speakers cover issues in growth and development. Spring speakers alternate between "growth and institutions," focusing on the macro aspects of growth and development, and "labor and development," focusing on the micro aspects. Students must complete both parts of this course (parts A and B) within the same academic year in order to receive credit.
