This course will cover a broad range of contemporary topics in origins of life research through a set of 10 two-week modules taught by members of the Origins of Life Initiative faculty.
Enrolled students will be required to attend at least six of the offered modules over the course of two semesters. The course will meet twice per week, and each two week module will be comprised of a stand-alone topic with a single assignment.
