Social Class in the United States: Identity, Culture, and Consciousness (126248)
Rachel Meyer
2018 Spring (4 Credits) Schedule: R 1100 AM - 1259 PM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: 10
This course will review a variety of empirical and theoretical perspectives on social class in the United States with a focus on class-based identities and class consciousness. This is a junior tutorial.
Course Notes: Required of and limited to Sociology concentrators. Junior Tutorials are by lottery only.
