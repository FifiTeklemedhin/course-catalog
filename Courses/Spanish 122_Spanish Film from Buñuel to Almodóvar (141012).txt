         This course will examine a wide range of core Spanish films from directors such as Almodóvar, Amenábar, Berlanga, Bollaín, Buñuel, Coixet, Erice, and León de Aranoa, among others. It will include analyses of prominent genres (drama, comedy, terror, fantasy, meta-cinema, sci-fi, noir) and will pay particular attention to key historical and cultural moments (the Spanish Civil War, film under dictatorship and democracy, censorship, "La movida," the economic crisis, the rise of immigration, etc.) from the 1940s to the present. Films with subtitles.
