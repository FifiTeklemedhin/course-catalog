Anthropology 2085
Archaeology of Ritual and Religion (128002)
Rowan Flad Matthew Liebmann
2018 Spring (4 Credits)
Instructor Permissions: None
Schedule: TBD Enrollment Cap: n/a
Graduate seminar exploring ritual and religious practices in archaeological contexts. Topics to be covered include anthropological perspectives on religion; origins of religion; religion and political economy; burial practices; materiality in/of ritual practice; and revitalization movements.
