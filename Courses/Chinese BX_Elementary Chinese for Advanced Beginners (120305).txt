Chinese BX
Elementary Chinese for Advanced Beginners (120305)
Emily Huang
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: MWF 1000 AM - 1059 AM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: n/a
For students with significant listening and speaking background. Introductory Modern Chinese language course, with emphasis on reading and writing. Covers in one term the equivalent of Chinese Ba and Bb.
No auditors. May not be taken Pass/Fail. Students must pass a test in listening and speaking to take the course.
Classes meet five days a week, additional discussion sections to be arranged.
