Thermal, synchrotron and spectral-line emission in the context of radio observations of the sun, planets, pulsars, masers, interstellar clouds, ionized regions, active galaxy central engines, quasars, and the cosmic background. Diffraction theory of antennas and interferometers. Signal propagation in space and Earth's atmosphere, detection, and measurement techniques. Observational and laboratory exercises that apply concepts treated in lectures and readings. Discussion of articles in the contemporary and historical literature.
