This course will provide students with a broad knowledge of central concepts, analytical tools and theoretical approaches for the study of public policy and public administration in different contexts. After reviewing core concepts related to public policy and the discipline(s) of policy analysis, the course will explore the impacts of actors and institutions on public policy and on public administration as well as the different stages of the policy process.
