Readings in Sa skya Pandita's (1182-1251) Sdom gsum rab dbye (205414)
Leonard W. J. van der Kuijp
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: R 0100 PM - 0359 PM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
Readings in Sa skya Pandita's (1182-1251) Sdom gsum rab dbye and its commentarial literature. This course will examine issues that relate to the three vows and the ways in which various authors chose to interpret them.
Recommended Prep: Must have two years of Tibetan or equivalent. Additional Course Attributes:
