A study of Zulu a major language spoken in South Africa at the Intermediate level (Second year part 2). Contact hours supplemented by digital resources sessions. Emphasis on written expression, reading comprehension, and oral fluency. Students must complete both terms of this course (parts BA and BB) within the same academic year in order to receive full Intermediate Zulu course credit. Students taking Zulu BA in the Spring must note that Zulu BB is offered only in the Spring.
