A study of Amharic the statutory national language and major lingua franca of Ethiopia at the Intermediate level (Second year part 1). Contact hours supplemented by language lab sessions. Emphasis on written expression, reading comprehension, and oral fluency. Students must complete both terms of this course (parts BA and BB) within the same academic year within the same academic year in order to receive full Intermediate Amharic course credit. Students taking Amharic BA in the Spring must note that Amharic BB is offered only in the Spring and must therefore wait for Spring to complete the course.
