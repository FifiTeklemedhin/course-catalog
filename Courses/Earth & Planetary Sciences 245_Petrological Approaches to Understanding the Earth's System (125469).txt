         The course studies the igneous processes at spreading centers, convergent margins and hot spots, and explores their influences on mantle, crust, ocean and atmosphere and the constraints they provide for the plate tectonic geochemical cycle.
Course Notes: Given in alternate years. Recommended Prep: EPS 145 or permission of instructor.
