 Moral Reasoning
             A comprehensive examination of Supreme Court decision making, aided by analysis of a computer databases on the court along with the statistical package STATA. The course covers various stages of the judicial process, including appointments to the Court, the decision to grant certiorari, the decision on the merits, majority-opinion assignment, and majority-opinion coalitions.
