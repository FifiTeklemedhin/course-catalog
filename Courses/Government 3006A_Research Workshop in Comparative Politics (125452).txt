             The workshop offers advanced graduate students an opportunity to present their work-in-progress, benefit from critiques of it, and discuss theoretical and methodological issues. Part one of a two part series. Students must complete both parts of this course (parts A and B) within the same academic year in order to receive credit.
Course Notes: Doctoral students from other departments and faculties admitted if space permits.
