History 1050
Slavery and the Slave Trade in the Atlantic World (204994)
Anne Ruderman
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: MW 1000 AM - 1059 AM Instructor Permissions: None Enrollment Cap: n/a
This course will explore the way the transatlantic slave trade andsubsequent systems of slavery in the Americas have shaped our modern world.Topics include the workings of the slave trade, the plantation complex, cropssuch as sugar, slavery outside of plantation economies, the evolution of racialideology, gender, rebellion, revolt, abolition and war. This course will make useof digital tools for student assignments and will offer students a basicintroduction to Geographic Information System (GIS) software andpodcasting.
