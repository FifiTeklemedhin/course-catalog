Bioethics: Seminar (124585)
Frances Kamm
2017 Fall (4 Credits) Schedule: W 0400 PM - 0559 PM Instructor Permissions: Instructor Enrollment Cap: n/a
The class will focus on philosophical issues about death and dying and their bearing on public policies. Readings will include recent books and articles by philosophers, medical professionals, and lawyers.
Course Notes: Offered jointly with the Harvard Kennedy School of Government as LAW- 90335A.
