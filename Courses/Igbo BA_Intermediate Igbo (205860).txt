Igbo BA
Intermediate Igbo (205860)
John Mugane
2017 Fall (4 Credits)
Instructor Permissions: None
Schedule: TBD Enrollment Cap: n/a
A study of Igbo one of the three most widely spoken languages in Nigeria at the Intermediate level (Second year part 1). Contact hours supplemented by language lab sessions. Emphasis on written expression, reading comprehension, and oral fluency. Students must complete both terms of this course (parts BA and BB) within the same academic year in order to receive full Intermediate Igbo course credit. Students taking Igbo BA in the Spring must note that Igbo BB is offered only in the Spring and must therefore wait for Spring to complete the course.
Course Notes: Languages in the tutorial program are offered when there is demonstrated curricular or academic need on the part of the student, and when suitable
instruction can be arranged. Please contact the department to learn more..
