This seminar course offers a survey in the following topics: the establishment of paradigms in astronomy; ethical behavior in astronomy; the underrepresentation of minorities and women in science; peer review and the scientific proposal process; the mentor-advisor relationship; professional conduct; careers in and beyond astronomy.
