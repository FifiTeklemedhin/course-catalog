Human Evolutionary Biology 1361
Hormones and Life History Physiology (203615)
Peter Ellison Susan Lipson
2018 Spring (4 Credits)
Instructor Permissions: Instructor
Schedule: Enrollment Cap:
M 0100 PM - 0229 PM n/a
A survey of human life history physiology and the role of hormones in the orchestration of key transitions, the allocation of energy to optimize fitness, and the consequences for human health and disease. Although the focus will be on humans, relevant comparisons with non-human primates and other vertebrates will also be made.
Lectures will be supplemented by reading and discussion of primary literature.
