         An introduction to philosophical and other accounts of meaning. Topics may include: human and non-human modes of communication; thought and reference; skepticism about meaning; vagueness; social meaning; truth and relativism.
Course Notes: Prerequisites: Two courses in philosophy or linguistics or permission of the instructor.
