         Examines literary theory and criticism inside the curriculum (trivium) and outside it (oral culture), manuscripts and commentary tradition, biblical exegesis, the arts of poetry, letter-writing, and preaching, Platonic and Aristotelian traditions, allegory/allegoresis, and sign theory. Writers examined include Augustine, Snorri, Dante, and Boccaccio.
